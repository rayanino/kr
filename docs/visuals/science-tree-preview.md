# Science Tree Preview

This page is a readable preview of the science trees that currently exist in KR.

It is based on the active tree files under `library/sciences/`, not on placeholder documentation. The goal here is not to print every last leaf, but to show the current shape of the library's subject map in a way the owner can react to.

## Active trees right now

- `العقيدة / Aqidah` -> active version `aqidah_v0_2`
- `النحو / Nahw` -> active version `nahw_v2_0`
- `الصرف / Sarf` -> active version `sarf_v1_0`
- `البلاغة / Balagha` -> active version `balagha_v1_0`
- `الإملاء / Imlaa` -> active version `imlaa_v1_0`

## 1. العقيدة / Aqidah

Current shape:

- Faith in Allah
  - Divine Names and Attributes
    - Example leaves: Method of Ahl al-Sunnah, Loftiness, Seeing Allah, Speech of Allah
    - Deeper branches include: Essential Attributes, Action Attributes
  - Divine Decree
    - Example leaves: Belief in Qadar, Stages of Qadar, Sects and Qadar
- Faith in the Last Day
  - Example leaves: Death and Barzakh, Resurrection and Gathering, Paradise and Hell
- Faith
  - Example leaves: Definition of Faith, Increase and Decrease of Faith
- Companions and Ahl al-Bayt
  - Example leaves: Virtues of the Companions, Ahl al-Bayt, Rank among the Companions
- Miracles of the Awliya
- Method of Ahl al-Sunnah wa al-Jama'ah
  - Example leaves: Following the Sunnah, Commanding Right and Forbidding Wrong, Staying with the Jama'ah

## 2. النحو / Nahw

Current shape:

- Foundations of Nahw and I'rab
  - Main areas: Speech and word types, I'rab and building, Signs of I'rab
- Nouns and definiteness
  - Main areas: Nakirah and Ma'rifah, Pronouns, Proper nouns, Demonstratives, Relative pronouns
- Nominal sentence and its sisters
  - Main areas: Mubtada and Khabar, Kana and her sisters, Inna and her sisters, La nafiyah lil-jins
- Verbal sentence and verb rulings
  - Main areas: Fa'il, deputy subject, transitive vs intransitive, Ishtighal, Tanaazu'
- Mansubat and attached functions
  - Main areas: Maf'ul bihi, Maf'ul mutlaq, Zarf, Hal, Tamyiz, Istithna'
- Jar and Idafah
  - Main areas: Harf al-jarr, Idafah
- Working nouns and derived forms
  - Main areas: verbal noun, active participle, passive participle, adjective-like forms, comparative forms
- Dependents and explanation
  - Main areas: Na't, Tawkid, 'Atf, Badal
- Special styles and special chapters
  - Main areas: Nida', Istighathah, Nadbah, Tarkhim, Ikhtisas, warning and urging styles, numbers, oaths

Owner reading note:

- Nahw is currently the broadest visible tree in KR.
- Its active version is the most explicitly marked as validated in the registry.

## 3. الصرف / Sarf

Current shape:

- Foundations of Sarf
  - Main areas: Definition of Sarf, core terminology, morphological scale, ilhaq, derivation
- Verb divisions
  - Main areas: Bare vs augmented, sound vs weak, passive formation
- Bare triliteral verb
  - Main areas: Verb patterns, sources, command form, active and passive participles
- Augmented triliteral verb
  - Main areas: One-letter increase, two-letter increase, three-letter increase, pattern meanings
- Bare and augmented quadriliteral verb
- Verb conjugation
  - Main areas: Past, present, command, attachment to pronouns
- Conjugation of sound forms
  - Main areas: Sound, hamzated, doubled forms
- Conjugation of weak forms
  - Main areas: Mithal, Ajwaf, Naqis, Lafif
- I'lal, Ibdal, Idgham, and metathesis
- Derived nouns
  - Main areas: verbal noun, active participle, passive participle, adjective-like forms, hyperbolic forms
- Noun-focused Sarf chapters
  - Main areas: Diminutive, Nisbah, broken and regular plurals, dual, Maqsur and Mamdud forms
- General issues and indexes

## 4. البلاغة / Balagha

Current shape:

- Foundations of Balagha
  - Main areas: Definition, subject, purpose, relation to other sciences, history
- Ilm al-Ma'ani
  - Main areas: Sentence structure, khabar, insha', qasr, word order, definiteness and indefiniteness, omission and mention, joining and separating, brevity and expansion
- Ilm al-Bayan
  - Main areas: Truth and metaphor, tashbih, majaz, isti'arah, kinayah and ta'rid
- Ilm al-Badi'
  - Main areas: Foundations of Badi', disputed chapters, meaning-based beautifiers, wording-based beautifiers

Owner reading note:

- Balagha is grouped in the classical three-part shape many students expect: Ma'ani, Bayan, and Badi'.
- Its active tree also keeps some disputed or school-dependent topics visible instead of hiding them.

## 5. الإملاء / Imlaa

Current shape:

- Foundations of spelling
  - Main areas: Definition of Imlaa, purpose, general writing rules
- Hamzah
  - Main areas: Hamzat al-wasl and hamzat al-qat', initial hamzah, middle hamzah, final hamzah
- Alif
  - Main areas: Soft alif, separating alif, tanwin al-fath alif
- Ta' and Ha' in writing
  - Main areas: Tied ta', open ta', ha' of pronoun
- Addition and deletion of letters
- Joined and separated writing
  - Main areas: What is written together, what is written separately
- Special words and names
  - Main areas: the name of Allah, ibn and bint, special exceptional spellings
- Modern writing marks
  - Main areas: punctuation marks and some modern writing conventions

## What this preview is for

The point of this page is to help the owner react to the current subject map:

- "Yes, this looks like the right big structure."
- "This branch feels too broad."
- "Why is this topic under that science?"
- "This science needs more detail here."

That kind of reaction is exactly what this preview is meant to support.
