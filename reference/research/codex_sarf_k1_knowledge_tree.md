## Organizational Principle

The tree is built on a classical-first order: define the science narrowly, establish its analytic tools, move through verbal morphology, then derivational nominals, then noun-side morphological classes, then morphophonemic operations, and end with a compact principles branch for analogy, transmitted usage, irregularity, and explanatory causes. The controlling rule throughout is that every node must remain about word form as a self-standing structure, not about syntactic position or sentence relations.

## 5 Strongest Branching Decisions

1. المدخل وحدود العلم was kept intentionally small so it orients the science without turning into a historical or metalinguistic dump branch.
2. الميزان والأصول والزيادة was separated from the actual verb and noun families so the analytic machinery of sarf remains visible and reusable across the rest of the tree.
3. المشتقات والأسماء المأخوذة من الأفعال was separated from الأبنية الاسمية وأحكامها الصرفية so derivation does not get mixed with number/gender/final-letter classes.
4. الممنوع من الصرف was retained only under form-based causes, while all case behavior and declensional discussion were excluded.
5. العمليات الصرفية والصوتية was limited to classical morphophonemic word-form changes, with الإمالة and الوقف confined to a narrow peripheral subgroup rather than promoted into broad branches.

## 5 Explicit Exclusions With Reasons

1. الإعراب was excluded because it tracks case and sentence-governed endings, whereas this tree is restricted to word-form structure.
2. العامل was excluded because it belongs to syntactic governance, not to the making and changing of word forms.
3. المبتدأ والخبر was excluded because it is a relation inside the sentence, not a morphological class.
4. الفاعل والمفعول به were excluded because they are syntactic roles even when particular forms may realize them.
5. التوابع مثل النعت والعطف والبدل were excluded because they are phrase-level and sentence-level relations, not sarf as the science of individual word formation.
