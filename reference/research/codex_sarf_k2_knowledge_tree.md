## Methodological Note

I chose the major branches by intersecting the five anchors instead of copying any single table of contents. What repeated across the canon was not one schoolbook layout, but a stable structure: boundary and definition, abstract pattern theory, applied verb morphology, applied noun/derivational morphology, internal transformational processes, and noun-specific transformations.

I prevented nahw bleed by applying a strict isolated-word test. If a topic becomes true only because the word occupies a syntactic slot, receives case/mood by an عامل, or is judged through sentence-level relations, I excluded it. If a topic belongs mainly to how the word is written, I also excluded it unless the point was unmistakably patternal and morphophonemic.

I refused to over-split in three places. I did not create an optional edge branch for همزة الوصل and similar liminal topics. I did not explode the rare additional verb patterns beyond the mainstream teachable weights. I also merged some tiny letter-specific subcases in الإبدال instead of turning every rare presentational split in one manual into a permanent leaf.

## Main Organizing Logic

- The tree moves from boundary to apparatus, then from apparatus to application.
- مدخل الصرف sets the science’s scope and guards the nahw boundary.
- الأبنية والأصول supplies the abstract machinery needed to read all later morphology.
- تصريف الأفعال and تصريف الأسماء والمشتقات carry the main applied instructional load.
- التغيرات الصرفية الداخلية captures operations that alter forms inside the word.
- التحويلات والأصناف الاسمية gathers noun-side transformations that are stable enough to study as their own morphological units.

## 5 Debatable Classification Choices

1. I included التثنية as sarf, but only as word-formation. I excluded all declensional and agreement behavior.
2. I included جمعا التصحيح on the same narrow basis: morphological formation stays in, syntactic case behavior stays out.
3. I kept اسم التفضيل under تصريف الأسماء والمشتقات because its formation is morphologically standard, while excluding its syntactic constructions with من and related usage.
4. I kept اللازم والمتعدي inside تصريف الأفعال as lexical-derivational verb properties, not as gateways to object syntax.
5. I excluded an edge branch for همزة الوصل, الإمالة, الوقف, and الابتداء even though some manuals mention them, because under the stricter boundary they do not form a clean, stable core branch.

## 5 Strongest Exclusions From Nahw/Imla Overlap

1. الممنوع من الصرف is excluded. Its operative force is declensional, not morphological.
2. الإعراب and all case/mood assignment are excluded. They belong to the word in syntax, not to the isolated word.
3. المبتدأ والخبر، الفاعل والمفعول، الحال، التمييز، التوابع، وأحكام الجملة are excluded as sentence-grammar topics.
4. رسم الهمزة، الألف اللينة، الوصل والفصل are excluded as imlaa, not sarf.
5. الوقف، الابتداء، الإمالة، والتقاء الساكنين are excluded as independent branches because they belong mainly to performance, junction, or mixed phonetic contexts rather than pure word-internal morphology.
